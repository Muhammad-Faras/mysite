PyAcademia Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Feb 20, 2024

Welcome to PyAcademia, a student-centric Django platform dedicated to fostering collaboration and learning among students. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information such as your name, email address, and profile picture when you register for an account.
  • Information you provide when completing your profile, including your skills, interests, and educational background.
  • Information generated from your interactions with the platform, such as posts, comments, messages, and forum discussions.
  • Information collected through cookies and similar tracking technologies when you access PyAcademia through a web browser.
  • Information obtained from third-party services if you choose to link your account with services like Google for authentication.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing and maintaining PyAcademia.
  • Personalizing your experience on the platform.
  • Communicating with you, including responding to inquiries and sending important notifications.
  • Improving our services and developing new features.
  • Conducting research and analysis to better understand user needs and preferences.
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service and other policies.

For the complete Privacy Policy, including information on data retention, security measures, third-party disclosures, and your rights regarding your personal information, please visit our full Privacy Policy page.
